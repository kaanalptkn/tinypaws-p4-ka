Adoption

Most of our rehomed pets aren't puppies or kittens. They're loud, quiet, fluffy, big, small, or even three-legged. They come with a personality ready-made, a past that's unique to them and a lot of love to give. Our animal frineds looking forward for your adoption. Click here to see our animal frineds


Our Adoption System:

  • You need to send request my email, filling out the form accordingly, make an appointment.

  • You should come to our shelter on the day and time of the appointment with adoption form and bringing following documents.

  • Photos that show the place where the dog or cat will live, including you

  • If you are going to own the house, residence showing the address registered in your name, water, wire, electricity bill, etc. and at least two references that can provide information about you.

  • Before coming to our shelter, you must have read all the texts on this page.

You should know before coming to our shelter to adopt our animal firends:

  1. The adoption system of our shelter is like a child welfare institution. Our dogs are abandoned animals. Since we do not want to cause such great pain to an animal that has experienced this trauma once, we need to get to know you, chat and be sure you are ready to adopt pets.

  2. Then, if necessary (if the video or photo is not enough), we see and examine your place closely. You have to go through the procedures like adopting a child from the same child welfare institution. Because they are like our children.

  3. If we are sure that they will not be abandoned again and that they will be very well taken care of, then we adopt them.

  4. As you can see in the form, since our shelter provides voluntary service, the family that adopts our dog has to help our shelter with necessary materials (dry food, wet food, pasta, medicine, vaccine, etc.) so that we can stand on our feet.

  5. We check our dogs at regular intervals after adopting them.

  6. Make sure to research if there are other shelters, vet clinics close to the area you live in.

  7. Our visiting hours are between 10:30 and 15:00, 5 days a week. You are kindly requested to come with an appointment for adoption.

  8. We are always waiting for our animal lover friends between 10.30-15:00 to adopt or spend time with our animal friends.
