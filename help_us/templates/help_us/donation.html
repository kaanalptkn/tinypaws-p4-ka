Donation

By shopping to Tinny Paws products, you can transform the lives of pets by helping to provide life-saving operations, paying for their food or finding loving new homes. Anything you will shop from our chairty it will help change the lives of thousands of pets across the UK.

Tinny Paws is the global animal welfare organisation for animals under direct human influence, which reveals suffering, rescues animals in need and protects them. Our vision is a world where humans treat animals with respect, empathy and understanding.

To do this we aim to create sustainable solutions for animals in need, touch hearts and change consumer behavior, drive legal change and build powerful partnerships.

The most effective way to protect animals over the long-term is to improve the legal framework. We attempt to achieve this by direct action and lobbying at the national and international level, and by appealing to the general public. We are internationally active, with our work not bound to any geographic limits.

We are NOT accepting direct donation, however all our t-shirts, hoodies, sweats, jeans, shoses are printed in the UK on premium quality extra soft organic cotton, made in an ethically accredited wind-powered factory.
